Manufacturing Workflow

Forging to Final Finish

This section is expanded from the home page process block. Every batch follows a controlled sequence for consistency in fitment, magnetic alignment, and long-term durability.

STEP 01

Forging

Mild steel low carbon billets are forged under controlled heat and force to achieve dense grain structure and durable base geometry before machining.

Output focus: structural strength and dimensional stability
STEP 02

Machining

CNC operations define bore, gap, and mating surfaces with tight tolerances so each part supports precise voice-coil alignment during assembly.

Output focus: tolerance control and consistent fit
STEP 03

Finishing

Parts are cleaned and coated in Chrome, CED, or Zinc according to model requirement, balancing corrosion protection, appearance, and production economics.

Output focus: corrosion resistance and surface quality
