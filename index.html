Who We Are

Built on the
Forge Floor

Celesti Craft is a precision speaker hardware manufacturer based in Morta, Ghaziabad — the industrial heartland of North India's audio components supply chain. We forge, machine, and finish pole plates, T-yokes and top plates for speaker manufacturers across India.

Every part is made from Mild Steel Low Carbon Billets, then CNC-machined to tolerance and finished in Chrome, CED or Zinc — giving your speaker motor the magnetic performance and surface quality it demands.

How It's Made

Manufacturing
Process

Every Celesti Craft part follows a disciplined three-stage process — forging builds strength, machining builds precision, and finishing builds durability.

01

Forging

Mild Steel Low Carbon Billets are processed for cast and 2-piece pole plate alternatives based on model, tolerance and application need, with control maintained across repeat batches.

02

Machining

CNC turning and boring to precise dimensional specs — bore diameter, face flatness, and gap geometry are held to tight tolerances for consistent voice coil alignment every batch.

03

Finishing

Parts are cleaned, treated and plated to your specification: bright Chrome, matte CED coating, or yellow Zinc passivation. Surface finish is inspected before packing.
